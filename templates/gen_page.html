A note on Discord character limits: I've included a warning when you go over 2000 characters in the output from this webpage, but you may reach the limit sooner than that with Discord emojis. Currently, Discord doesn't do a great job of warning you when you exceed the character limit with this bubble-wrap-type text. If you send your message and a bot called Clyde tells you it couldn't be delivered, try chopping a row off the end and trying again. (Each custom emoji gains 21 extra unseen characters once you send it, compared to the :emojiname: version you see when you type it. You can do the math if you want to have a precisely-calibrated-for-maximium-size piece of custom bubblewrap.)
